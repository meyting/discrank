Your task

In this part, you will be asked to choose between two workers.

You will make 9 decisions. In each decision, you have to choose between two workers. For each worker, you will see information about: A) whether they performed in the top 4, middle 4 or bottom 4 within their set from 1-{{ C.number_of_workers_sep }} and B) their gender (shown by an avatar).

Your bonus payment

If this part will be drawn as the part-that-counts, one of your decisions will also be randomly drawn as the decision-that-counts. Your bonus payment is determined by the performance of the worker that your hired in the decision-that-counts. For each point that your assigned worker has, you receive {{ C.conversionrate }}. Example: If that worker who you hired in the decision-that-counts got a productivity score of {{ C.examplescore }}, you will receive a bonus payment of {{ C.examplebonus}}.
