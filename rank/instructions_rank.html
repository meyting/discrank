Your bonus payment

If this part will be drawn as the part-that-counts, your bonus payment is determined by the performance of the worker that is allocated to you. For each point that your assigned worker has, you receive {{ C.conversionrate }}.

In total, {{C.number_of_other_subjects}} other participants will also all do the ranking that you are doing. Which worker is allocated to you, depends on your ranking and the ranking of the other participants.

After all {{ C.number_of_workers_tot }} participants, have ranked the workers, we will randomly assign a number between 1-{{ C.number_of_workers_tot }} to each participant. This number determines your position of when you can pick a worker.

Example: After the study is completed by all participants, we assign you the number 4. Thus, your worker choice will be the worker that ranks highest in your ranking and which is still available after the first 3 other participants have chosen their worker. If that worker got a productivity score of {{ C.examplescore }}, you will receive a bonus payment of {{ C.examplebonus}}.
