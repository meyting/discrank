Your bonus payment

In total, {{C.number_of_other_subjects}} other participants will also all rank the same workers as you. After all participants have ranked the workers, we will randomly assign a number between 1 and {{ C.number_of_workers_tot }} to each participant. This number determines your position of when a worker will be allocated to you.

The person with the number 1 gets their top-ranked worker (rank 1). The chosen worker is then not available anymore. The person with the number 2 gets their top-ranked worker unless this worker has already been allocated before, in which case they get the second-ranked worker, etc.. This means that the worker allocated to each participant is always their respective best-ranked worker who is still available.

If this part will be drawn as the part-that-counts, you will receive {{ C.conversionrate }} for each point of the productivity score of the worker that has been allocated to you.

Example: After the study is completed by all participants, we assign you the number 4. Thus, the worker that will be allocated to you will be the worker that ranks highest in your ranking and that is still available after the first 3 other participants have been allocated their worker. If that worker got a productivity score of {{ C.examplescore }}, you will receive a bonus payment of {{ C.examplebonus}}.
