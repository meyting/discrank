Your bonus payment

In this study, you get the chance to earn a bonus payment. This study consists of two main parts. At the end of this experiment, one of these two parts will be randomly selected as the "part-that-counts". The bonus that you earned in the "part-that-counts" will be paid out to you. The two parts are independent from each other, meaning that the decisions in part I do not affect part II.
